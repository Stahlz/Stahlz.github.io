Good Day Fellow InfoSec Folk

I am Joshua Whitaker. Currently i am working as a Incident Responder in HealthCare, and i have experience in threat intel, threat hunting, and security automation The main objective of this blog is to provide walkthroughs, tips, tricks on setting up a non-attributinal network. As well as general information related to privacy and security.

Why would you ever need a Non-Attributional network?

Maybe you need to do research on an external host, and don't want to tip off an attacker by showing your public ip address. Your a pentester who wants to conceal their identity, for a campaign your running. Even someone who's just privacy concious about goverment surveillance, and wants to decrease their digital footprint.
